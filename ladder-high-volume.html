CSP Readiness Checklist

  • How much cash is available to sell puts.

    Where in TOS: Monitor → Account Statement → Cash & Sweep Vehicle.

  • Decide what % of capital to deploy this week.

    Where in TOS: Personal target (not in TOS).

  • Choose strikes with delta between 0.14–0.17.

    Where in TOS: Trade → Option Chain → Delta column.

  • Choose expirations around 7–12 days.

    Where in TOS: Trade → Option Chain → Expiration column.

  • Look for tickers pulling back but not oversold.

    Where in TOS: Charts → Studies → RSI.

  • Higher IV = higher premium; avoid extremely low IV.

    Where in TOS: Trade → Option Chain → IV column.

  • Choose strikes with at least 79% probability OTM.

    Where in TOS: Trade → Option Chain → Prob OTM column.

  • Do not sell CSPs into earnings.

    Where in TOS: Charts → Earnings icon OR MarketWatch → Calendar → Earnings.

If Position Is ITM

  • Close the CSP if the underlying trades below your strike AND delta rises above 0.30. This indicates the position is no longer a high‑probability winner and risk is increasing.

    Where in TOS: Monitor → Positions → Right‑click → Create Closing Order.

  • Example: If Delta > 0.28 AND Theta < 2.0, consider closing or rolling early.

    Rising delta means increasing assignment risk; falling theta means the trade is no longer paying you enough to justify holding.

    Where in TOS: Monitor → Positions → Add Delta and Theta columns.
